We assist realtors in marketing their unique brand and listings.

By Jodelle, Ltd. helps real estate clients define themselves and their marketing strategy and set and achieve sales goals through several tools including digital marketing and print advertising.  We also assist you in marketing your listing.  We offer a variety of digital resources, including real estate photography and videography, and can help make the property show-ready through home staging and coaching. The goal is to ensure the listing stand out, to create a vivid and modern marketing strategy that lingers in the minds of buyers and to reinforce your branding all at the same time. The care that you give your listing today will help garner referrals for years to come.
